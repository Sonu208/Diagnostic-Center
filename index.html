Diagnostic centers form a very important part of the health infrastructure. Proper diagnosis is instrumental to ensure timely and effective treatment. The presence or absence of quality diagnostic centers in cities or suburbs can indicate the quality of treatment. The diagnostic market in India is growing, especially in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities. Tier 1 cities already have a sufficient number of diagnostic centres. No. of general pathology diagnostic centers is growing at the rate of 20% and specialized pathology like biopsy, genetics, oncology, genomic sequencing is growing at 30-35% and is expected to grow for another 3-4 years. Our team has of 200 senior pathologists and over 2000 technicians delivering diagnostic solutions in the areas of routine, semi specialty and super specialty domains like Oncology, Neurology, Gynaecology, Nephrology and many more..
